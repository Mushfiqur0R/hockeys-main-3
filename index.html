How are new hockey sticks made?

To manufacture the hockey sticks, long sheets of composite materials are wrapped around molds of a blade or shaft, allowing a machine to compress the material until it forms the intended design.

How is hockey stick tape made?

Hockey tape is typically made of cloth, fabric, or a combination of materials. It has a textured surface with a tacky adhesive that provides grip and control.

Do I need a new hockey stick?

When a stick feels “whippy” or weak in shooting or passing, many players will buy a new stick because the old one isn't performing like it should. It's worn out—a factor that a novice may not even notice.

What is composite hockey stick?

These hockey sticks are made from carbon fiber sheets impregnated with partially cured resin. Composite shafts are made by fusing up to 15 layers of carbon fiber sheets using epoxy resin.

What is the hockey stick trend?

A hockey stick chart is a chart characterized by a sharp increase after a relatively flat and quiet period.
